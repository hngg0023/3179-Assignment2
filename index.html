Global YouTube Statistics
As YouTube getting more famous nowadays, people can easily become a Youtuber and earn money from this platform. The main reason said by Moshin in 2023 is that "People enjoy watching videos on Youtube everyday". Thus, the views of each video will turns to an equivalent value of money for the content creator. It is clearly shows on the Choropleth Map, for every 100 people in places like Canada, Australia, Saudi Arabia, and Ukraine, the total video viewed have reached 10 Thousands and above.
There are many types of channel in Youtube all around the world. However, in the Packed Bubble Chart below, it is categorizing the channels into few big category like Entertainment, Music, People, Games, Education, and etcetera. The size of the bubble is showing the total video views for each channel and the sequential colour is representing the money earned by the content provider in each category.
Although Youtube is providing an easy platform for content provider to upload videos and attract viewers, there are too many competitor in the same category. Therefore, less people chose not to be a content provider. As shown in the Bar Chart below, the Number of Youtuber is dropping since 2017. Furthermore, when a specific year is selected, the Number of Youtubers in each specific category will be shown.
As mentioned above, the Number of Youtuber is dropping and the scatter plot shows the actual cause. As shown on the right side scatter plot, content provider had once earned 14 Million dollar monthly during the peak. However, due to the current economy, with the same number of views on their video, it is now possible for them to earn around 850 Thousand dollar only.
